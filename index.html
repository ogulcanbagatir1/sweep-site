Frequently asked

I removed something by mistake — can I get it back?

Yes. Sweep sends every removal to the Trash first, never a direct delete. Open History inside Sweep and choose Undo, or restore the item from the Trash. This works for 30 days.

Is it safe? What gets deleted?

Sweep labels every item Safe, Caution, or Protected with a plain-English reason, and never touches the files you created. You always confirm before anything is removed.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Go to System Settings → your Apple ID → Media & Purchases → Subscriptions, select Sweep, and cancel. You keep Pro until the period ends.

I already bought Pro — how do I restore it?

Open the Sweep Pro screen and tap Restore Purchase. Make sure you’re signed in with the same Apple ID you bought it with.

What’s free and what needs Pro?

Scanning, the safety labels, and 30-day Undo are free forever. Sweep Pro unlocks unlimited one-click cleaning, the complete app uninstaller, scheduled scans, and menu-bar quick clean.

Which macOS versions are supported?

macOS 14 (Sonoma) and later, including macOS 26 (Tahoe).
