✦Sweep

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Sweep is a Mac disk cleaner that runs entirely on your device. It is built on a simple principle: your files are yours. Sweep does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, and it has no servers of its own.

The short version: No accounts. No tracking. No analytics. No data leaves your Mac.

What Sweep accesses

To find and free up space, Sweep reads information about files on your Mac — their size, location, and age — and, when you choose to remove something, moves it to the Trash. This all happens locally. Sweep never uploads, copies off-device, or shares the contents of your files.

Data collection

None. Sweep contains no analytics, no advertising, no third-party trackers, and no telemetry. It does not ask you to create an account or sign in.

Purchases

Sweep Pro is sold through Apple’s App Store. Purchases, subscriptions, and any billing information are handled entirely by Apple — Sweep never sees your payment details. You can manage or cancel a subscription anytime in System Settings → your Apple ID → Media & Purchases → Subscriptions. Apple’s handling of purchase data is covered by Apple’s own Privacy Policy.

Children

Sweep is a general-purpose utility and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
